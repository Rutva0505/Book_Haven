Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major PayPal, GPay, UPI payments, bank transfers, and all other online means of payments. We only accept online means of payments.

How do I find a specific book?

Use our search bar at the top of the page. Enter the title, author, or ISBN number to find the book you're looking for. You can also browse by category or author through our navigation menu.

Do you offer original copies of these books?

No, we don't offer original copies of these books as it doesn't signify our code of conduct.

Do you offer scratch cards?

Yes, we offer scratch cards in various denominations. You can purchase them directly from our website and they can be used for any purchase.

How do I update my account information?

To update your account information, log in to your account and go to the "Account Settings" section. From there, you can update your personal details and payment methods.

How do I subscribe to your newsletter?

You can subscribe to our newsletter by entering your email address in the subscription box at the bottom of our homepage. You'll receive updates on new arrivals, promotions, and events.

How can I provide feedback or suggestions?

We welcome your feedback and suggestions. Please email us at feedback@bookstore.com or use the contact form on our website to share your thoughts.
